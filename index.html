The Taiping Rebellion: A Timeline

The taiping Rebellion cost between 20 and 70 million lives, making it one of the deadliest conficts in human history. Though underrepresented in modern historical discourse, this rebellion signalled the death knell of the Qing Empire and Imperial China writ large.

Skiing on Vale Mountain

Skiing is an important part of everyday life for the residents of Copper and Vale Mountains. Many small towns dot interstate 70 going through the Rocky Mountain range and they play host to seasonal sports tourists. This long tourism season requires ski supply and rental shops to dot the mountainside, providing tourists with ski rentals and equipment purchases. Those with more experience are also able to upgrade or maintain their personal equipment in most of these shops.

Power Plants in the U.S.

This map represents Solar, Wind, Hydroelectric, and Natural Gas energy plants across the United States. Natural gas and coal still provide the second and third largest fuel sources in the country respectively, following oil. Even so, the Grand Coulee Dam in Washington State is the largest energy producer in the country, providing almost 8,000 Megawatts of power to the surrounding arounding area.

New England Natural Bridge

This natural bridge, located in Natural Bridge State Park of North Adams, Massachusetts. This bridge is made of white marble, a feature unique to North America. The bridge spans the Hudson brook and was developed through glacial erosion. The surrounding area has historically been used for white marble mining.

Archaeology of Northern Ireland

Northern Ireland is a hotbed for archaeological activity. Because of the rich, continuous cultural history presented in the soil archaeologists flock to the area to conduct field research. Northern Ireland is also one of the preeminent areas wherein field schools are conducted. The rich and many-times undisturbed monuments are a great place for budding archaeologists to learn the ins and outs of excavation and the things one might be expected to find when studying in Western Europe. Many different periods are studied in and around Northern Ireland from the Roman Invasion to the troubles of the early 90s.

Points of Interest in New Orleans

New Orleans is one of the most vibrant, colorful cities in the world. It is home to the New Orleans Saints and the NOLA Pelicans. It is also the center of Cajun cuisine and boasts traditional french-creole restaurants throughout the city. It is considered a destination for everything entertainment, with it's motto being "Laissez les bon temps rouler". In New Orleans, they certainly let the good times roll.
